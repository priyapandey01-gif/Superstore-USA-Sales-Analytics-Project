## 📁 Dataset
- **Source:** Sample Superstore (Tableau)
- **Orders:** 9,994 | **Period:** 2014–2017
- **Regions:** 4 | **States:** 49 | **Categories:** 3


## 💡 Key Findings
- 📊 Technology = top category (36.4% of sales)
- 📉 Tables sub-category = biggest loss maker
- 👥 Consumer segment = 50.56% of revenue
- 🌍 West region = highest sales ($725.5K)
- 📅 Sales peak every November–December
- 💸 High discounts = lower profit margins
- 🔄 Return rate = 5.91% | Profit Margin = 12.47%


## 📈 Recommendations
- ✅ Reduce discounting on low margin products
- ✅ Focus on Technology category
- ✅ Fix return issues in Furniture
- ✅ Grow presence in Central region
- ✅ Target Consumer segment for campaigns
